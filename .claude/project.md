\# Digital Twin Project Instructions



Build a voice-first medical digital twin MVP.



Priorities:

1\. Communication quality over fancy UI.

2\. Use patient context from Synthea JSON.

3\. Answer like a calm voice assistant.

4\. Explain medical reasoning simply.

5\. Never invent citations.

6\. When uncertain, say what is uncertain and suggest what data is needed.

7\. For every health recommendation, include why it matters.

8\. Keep answers short enough for speech.

9\. Add evidence/references when available.

10\. Make the demo feel trustworthy, personal, and safe.
